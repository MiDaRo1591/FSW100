Burning Tower is a game setting withing the fictional world of Te'har. You play as an adventurer that has traveled to the Lligimore Islands, withing the borders of the Deadlands. Players are encouraged to join Guilds and form Clans and to take over Districts that gather Valuable Rescorces. The world is set as a PvE and PvP with several Dungeons and Raids that can be joined.

The game is class free, instead players start by choosing a Race, then gain Abilities and Skills to for roles that they take in Combat such as Heavy Armor and Shields, as well as various Weapons and Magic spells. As well as the game features various Crafting Roles, including Metalurgy, Carpentry, Textiles, and Potions, to name a few.

Multiplayer PvP Rules can be toggled mostly on the fly, however certant Districts have pre-set Rules that cannot be toggled. 1v1 Duals and 2v1 to 10v10 Spars can be taken with the intuitive Challenge System. Large groups can travel to the Arena of Harbinger where they can face mob challenges, or take part in massive open ended Melee's to earn coins and increase Renowned.
