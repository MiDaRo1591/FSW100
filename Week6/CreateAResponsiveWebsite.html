Abilities

Your core abilities influence your skills and various game mechanics as you travel the Lligimore Islands districts. From bases under your command, the ability to hit targets, gear you carry, and how quickly you recover from poisons and infections.

Physique

This represents Physical Strength, Toughness, and endurance. Higher levels increase Carry Weight, Overall Health, and allows Heaver Weapons and Armor to be used.

Dexterity

This represents Hand Eye Coordination, Agility, and Speed. Higher levels increase Chance to Dodge, Ability to Hit, and Movement Speed.

Vitality

This represents Stamina, Resistance, and Health. Higher levels Increases Fatigue Recovery, Health Regeneration, and Infection Recovery.

Willpower

This represents Astuteness, Force of Intent, and Presents. Higher levels increase Spell Capacity Command, and Ki.
