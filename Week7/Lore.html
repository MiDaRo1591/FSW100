Deadlands (Orrin)

* Islands
  • Pinnacle Rock

    • With the only village of Harbor Town, Pinnacle Rock is composed of a curved ridge running the length of the island. This ridge has several caves rich in saltpeter can be found in abundance.

  • Subrosin

    • Within the island of Subrosin Coagulate, Diverge, and Ichor Each of which is protected by forts.

    Coagulate is protected by the ancient Lorals Fortress though it has fallen into decay. While rooms are in disrepair, the ramparts and walls are constantly repaired by the Adventurer's to hold the area, and thus gather the Copper, Zink, and Tin in the area.

    Diverge is protected by the river Krozess, which divides the island in half, and the Primus Keep. The huge woodlands to the north and swamps to the east provide ample supplys of wood.
